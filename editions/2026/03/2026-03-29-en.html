🔍Zalando Uses AI as a Judge for Search Quality Assurance

Zalando engineers shared a detailed look at how they use LLM-as-a-judge to evaluate search quality when launching in new markets. By orchestrating the process with Airflow and using GPT-4o to score search results via NER (Named Entity Recognition), they've automated a complex QA task that previously required heavy manual effort. This is a prime example of LLMs being integrated into production-grade data pipelines.
