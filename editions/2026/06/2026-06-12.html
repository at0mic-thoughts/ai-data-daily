Anthropic shipped Claude Fable with undisclosed 'distillation guardrails' that silently blocked certain outputs without telling users or developers. 🤗 HuggingFace Ships Open Reproduction of DeepSeek-R1

Open-R1 is a fully open, auditable reproduction of DeepSeek-R1 from HuggingFace, covering training code, data, and weights. This matters because it decouples reasoning capability from closed vendors and makes self-hosted reasoning pipelines a real option for data teams with compliance constraints.
